About Us

Campus Lost & Found is a simple system designed to help students and staff report and recover lost items quickly and securely.

What we do

  • Allow users to report lost or found items.
  • Help match items using clear descriptions and location details.
  • Track item status (Active, Claimed, Resolved).

Why it matters

A centralized system reduces confusion and improves the chances of returning items to their owners.
